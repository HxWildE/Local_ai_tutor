But rerankers use a Cross-Encoder.
Instead of separate vectors, they process both texts together.

Input to reranker:
[Question + Chunk]

Example input:
Question: How are CNNs used in computer vision?
Chunk:
Convolutional neural networks are widely used in image classification.

The model reads both at once and outputs a score like:
Relevance score = 8.2

This is much more accurate.
