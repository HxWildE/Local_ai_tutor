Problem with L2 distance?

Sentence embeddings are about direction, not magnitude.
Cosine similarity measures:

Which basically means:  How aligned are the vectors, Not how far apart in space.
Same direction, different length в†’ Cosine = high similarity
Different direction в†’ Cosine = low similarity

For sentence embeddings: (Cosuine Similarity Range)
0.85+ в†’ Very similar
0.70вЂ“0.85 в†’ Good match
0.50вЂ“0.70 в†’ Weak
< 0.50 в†’ Probably unrelated

How it happens in FAISS:
FAISS does NOT directly compute cosine.

Instead:
Normalize vectors (make length = 1)  and Use inner product

When vectors are normalized:   Inner Product = Cosine Similarity

So:  IndexFlatIP  +  normalized vectors  = cosine similarity search
